What services does 3M Car Care Studio in Bangalore offer?

We provide paint protection, ceramic & graphene coatings, sun control films, interior cleaning, anti-corrosion treatment, car wraps, custom styling, and premium floor mats.

What is 3M Paint Protection Film (PPF)?

PPF is a transparent, durable film that protects your car’s paint from scratches, stone chips, UV damage, and daily wear while maintaining its glossy finish.

How long does 3M Paint Protection Film last?

With proper care, 3M PPF can last 5–7 years, depending on coverage and environmental exposure.

What is the benefit of 3M Sun Control Film?

It reduces heat, glare, and UV rays, protecting your car interior, enhancing driving comfort, and keeping passengers safe from harmful sun exposure.

What is 3M Graphene Coating?

A nano-technology coating that provides superior durability, hydrophobic properties, and enhanced paint shine, making cleaning easier and extending paint life.

How is 3M Ceramic Coating different from PPF?

Ceramic coating provides a hard protective layer for paint gloss and minor scratch resistance, while PPF offers physical protection against chips, scratches, and impact.

What is 3M Interior GermKleen Treatment?

It is an antimicrobial treatment that eliminates bacteria, viruses, allergens, and odors, keeping your car interior clean and hygienic.

How often should I get Interior GermKleen Treatment?

For optimal hygiene, it is recommended every 6–12 months, depending on usage and exposure.

What is 3M Anti-Corrosion Treatment?

This treatment protects your car’s underbody, chassis, and vulnerable areas from rust, corrosion, moisture, and road salts.

What are 3M Car Wraps & Custom Styling?

Premium car wraps and custom designs that enhance your vehicle’s appearance while protecting the original paint, available in gloss, matte, satin, and textured finishes.

How long does a 3M Car Wrap last?

A professionally installed 3M wrap typically lasts 5–7 years, depending on care and environmental conditions.

What are 3M Nomad Floor Mats?

Custom-fit, durable floor mats designed to protect your car’s interior from dirt, spills, mud, and daily wear while adding a stylish look.

Can 3M coatings and films be applied to any car type?

Yes, 3M services are suitable for all types of vehicles, including sedans, SUVs, sports cars, and luxury vehicles.

How long does it take to install 3M Paint Protection Film?

Installation depends on coverage but typically takes 1–3 days for full-body application.

Are 3M Car Care products safe for my vehicle’s paint and interior?

Yes, all 3M products are tested and certified to be safe, durable, and non-damaging to your car’s paint, glass, and interior surfaces.
