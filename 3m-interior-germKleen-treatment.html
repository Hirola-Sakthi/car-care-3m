3M Interior GermKleen Treatment

Keep Your Car Interior Clean, Fresh, and Germ-Free

Protect your vehicle’s interior and the health of passengers with 3M Interior GermKleen Treatment — a specialized antimicrobial treatment designed to eliminate bacteria, viruses, and other harmful microorganisms. This advanced treatment ensures your car’s cabin remains hygienic, fresh, and safe, especially in high-traffic urban areas like Bangalore.

Healthy, Safe, and Odor-Free Interior

At 3M Car Care Studio – Bangalore, our trained technicians apply the GermKleen treatment meticulously to all interior surfaces, including seats, dashboards, air vents, carpets, and door panels. The antimicrobial solution penetrates deep into fabric and leather surfaces, reducing the risk of germs while neutralizing unpleasant odors. It’s an essential service for families, professionals, and anyone who spends long hours in their car.
